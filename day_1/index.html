Dive into My Music Taste

Read time: 5 mintues

My Favoruite genres

Indie rock, bedroom pop, alternative rock, pop punk, emo, electropop, art pop, post-hardcore, metalcore, lo-fi, K-pop, R&B, trap, rage rap, cloud rap, hyperpop, gothic rock, alternative metal, synthwave, dream pop, ambient pop, UK rap, hip-hop, bubblegum pop, electro-pop, psychedelic rap, pop rap, and country.

My music taste leans heavily towards emotionally charged, alternative sounds with a strong sense of individuality. I am drawn to the melodic, heartfelt rock of artists like Beabadoobee and Paramore. Melanie Martinezs quirky, conceptual pop with its darker undertones really resonates with me. I also love the intensity and emotional rawness of bands like Pierce the Veil and Violent Vira. On the flip side, I enjoy the dreamy indie vibe of TV Girl and the smooth, nostalgic allure of Kiss of Life, which add depth to my eclectic playlist.

"Music is the only thing that can make me feel alive" Hali, 2024

My music taste is a blend of diverse genres, with a strong inclination towards artists who bring emotional depth and innovation to their work. I am drawn to the smooth, introspective vibes of Keshi, SZA, and Bryson Tiller, and I appreciate the raw energy in the music of Yeat, Ken Carson, and Destroy Lonely. I enjoy the dreamy and atmospheric sounds of Cigarettes After Sex and Whirr, while also vibing with the experimental edge of Bladee and PinkPantheress. My playlist spans everything from nostalgic pop with Hilary Duff to the sophisticated pop of Taylor Swift and the edgy rock of Evanescence. The fusion of R&B, alternative, and pop elements in artists like The Weeknd, Poppy, and Mitski perfectly encapsulates my love for both emotional resonance and creative exploration across various musical landscapes.

"LIVE LOVE LAUGH MUSIC" Hali, 2020
